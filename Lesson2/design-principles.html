This website is a good example of "Visual Hierarchy" your eye is drawn to the the order of what is more important.

  1. The image of the Might Ducks team on the zamboni
  2. The title of the show.
  3. "Stream Now" button so it is easy for the viewer to start watching the movie.
  4. Description of the series.
  5. The links to other pages on the website.
